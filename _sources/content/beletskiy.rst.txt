Beletskiy, Irina and Sergei
===================================

Irina Beletskaya and Sergei Beletskiy - a married couple who have jointly conducted soconics teaching courses and counseling. Associated with Center of Practical Socionics and Psychology.

Irina Beletskaya has graduated from the Moscow Power Engineering Institute (MEI), the department of automation and computer engineering, and attends additional courses from the Moscow institute of economics, politics, and law. She was first introduced to socionics in 2002. By the end of 2004 and in 2005 she studied socionics in a group led by Liubov Beskova. In 2006 she published her first articles on socionics. Creator and leader of the Center of Practical Socionics and Psychology - NewTraining, where she has been working, leading training courses and consultations. Since 2008 participates in the development and research on the semantics of small groups, and the link between socionics TIMs and human genotype. Self typings: LIE

Sergei Beletski has graduated from Kharkov State University with a diploma of "physicist, astronomer, professor of physics and astronomy", which he has also attended for his graduate degree. Receives additional education from the Moscow institute of economics, politics, and law. In 2001 - 2003 attended training in Sinton, earning the certificate of Sinton master. In 2004 - 2005 attended training in NLP. Was first introduced to socionics in 2001. Published his first aricles in 2005. Since 2006 works in the Center of Practical Socionics and Psychology - NewTraining.Ru as a consultant and organizer of courses in socionics together with his wife Irina. Self typings: ESI.
