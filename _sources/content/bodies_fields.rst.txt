Bodies and fields
=========================

**Bodies** and **fields** are concepts from physics which were used by :doc:`Aushra Augusta <augusta>` as an allegory to elaborate the difference between :doc:`extraverted <extroverted>` and :doc:`introverted <introverted>` information aspects. These days it is more common to see "objects" and "relationships" instead of bodies and fields.
