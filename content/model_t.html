Model T is a psychophysiological model proposed by Viktor Talanov as an alternative to Model A. The model is based on the idea of perceptual thresholds and employs 4 psychic functions instead of 8 as in Model A.

Model T explains some Reinin traits which are otherwise difficult to understand:

  • Emotivist / Constructivist

  • Tactical / Strategic

  • Yielding / Obstinate

  • Carefree / Farsighted

  • Model T and Reinin Traits by V. Talanov

  • Comparison of Model T and Model A by V. Talanov
